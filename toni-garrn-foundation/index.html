About

Can technology expand meaningful access to education?

Artificial intelligence is rapidly reshaping how societies create opportunity — and how access to education can be scaled.

For decades, educational impact has depended on institutions, funding structures, and physical infrastructure. Today, AI introduces a new layer: intelligence systems that can support learning, coordination, and decision-making at scale.

This private Evening Salon brings together founders, executives, and partners of the Toni Garrn Foundation to explore one central question — how can technology expand meaningful access to education without losing its human core?

This is not about tools. It is about systems, responsibility, and long-term impact.
