Narrative Power

Brand, Reputation & Influence in the Age of AI

Hosted by Alexandra von Rehlingen & Franziska Wittleder

Private Gathering · By Invitation

About

Artificial intelligence is transforming the way brands are built, stories are told, and influence is created.

For decades, reputation was shaped through relationships, media, institutions, and carefully crafted narratives. Today, AI is fundamentally changing the communication landscape — enabling the creation, distribution, personalization, and amplification of narratives at unprecedented scale.

This private Salon AI evening brings together founders, brand leaders, communicators, investors, and executives to sit with the tension underneath all the others: how do you stay trustworthy — and still reach the world?

Themes to Discuss

Trust as the Ultimate Scarce Resource

As synthetic content becomes ubiquitous, trust may become the most valuable asset a brand can possess.

How do organizations build credibility in an increasingly automated information environment?

Reputation at Machine Scale

What happens when every company has access to AI-powered communication infrastructure?

Will reputation become more important — or harder to establish?

The Future of Brand Building

How do brands remain distinctive when content creation becomes nearly infinite?

What creates enduring brand value in a world saturated with AI-generated communication?

Founder Brands & Executive Visibility

AI is lowering the barriers to content creation. Does that increase or decrease the importance of authentic leadership?

What role will founders and executives play in shaping brand narratives?
